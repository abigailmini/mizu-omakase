Our Philosophy お任せ

Omakase —
"I leave it up to you."

At Mizu Omakase, the multi-course menu is chosen entirely by our Head Chef, guided by the rhythm of the seasons and the finest ingredients sourced that morning. Served at our twenty-seat Japanese restaurant, each course unfolds with grace, precision, and an unwavering respect for tradition.

You are not a diner here. You are a guest at the counter, watching the work unfold, hand by hand, course by course.

sixteen movements.

The menu changes nightly — what follows is a glimpse of the atmosphere, not a spoiler. The cadence, the textures, the rhythm of fire and ice. Your meal will be written on the evening of your visit.

The Chef · 職 人

Head Chef
A study in quiet mastery.

大将 · Itamae

Our head chef trained beneath traditional itamae in both Tokyo and Osaka before returning to Malaysia to pursue his own expression of Edomae. His hand is unhurried, his cuts deliberate, his seasoning almost imperceptibly exact — a practice refined across two decades at the counter.

At Mizu, he sources from Toyosu auctions twice weekly, pairing Japanese catches with the best of the region's seasonal bounty. The result is omakase that belongs, unmistakably, to this place — and to this moment.

"Every guest receives the same season. Yet no two meals are ever the same."

Before Your Visit · ご 案 内

A few notes for the evening.

Because the meal is crafted in real time at an intimate counter, we ask guests to plan ahead and arrive with an open appetite.

What is the price of the omakase?

＋
The evening menu is priced at RM 688++ per guest for the full seasonal omakase (16+ courses). Seasonal truffle, caviar, and wagyu additions are offered at the counter.

What is the dress code?

＋
Smart casual, with a request for closed shoes. We kindly ask guests to avoid strong perfumes, as they interfere with the subtle seasonal aromas of the menu.

How long is the experience?

＋
Approximately 2 to 2½ hours. Two seatings run nightly — 6:00 PM and 8:30 PM — Tuesday through Sunday.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

＋
Please let us know at least 48 hours before your reservation. We can accommodate most allergies and restrictions, though vegetarian and vegan omakase must be arranged in advance.

May I bring my own sake or wine?

＋
Corkage is available at RM 150 per bottle, limited to two bottles per party. Our own list features 14 sakes and a carefully paired progression.

What is the cancellation policy?

＋
We hold a credit card at booking. Cancellations within 48 hours incur the full omakase charge, as ingredients are sourced specifically for your seat.
